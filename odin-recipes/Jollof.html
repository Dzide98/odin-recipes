Jollof

DESCRIPTION

Jollof Rice is a popular West African rice dish,eaten at parties and other ceremonies in Ghana,Nigeria,Senegal and some other countries.

INGREDIENTS

  • blended pepper
  • 2 large sliced spring onions
  • 1 teaspoon garlic
  • 4 teaspoons tomato
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 600 grams of rice rinsed and drained
  • 1 large carrot peeled and cut into small cubes
  • 4.6 cups vegetable stock
  • 5 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • salt
